About

Built from a

The BBS RS is one of the most iconic wheels ever bolted to a car. The spoke geometry, the hardware, the way light catches a polished lip — it all holds up. I got into refinishing because I wanted my own sets done right, and it turned into something else entirely.

Every commission starts with a conversation about what you're building, what look you're chasing, and what the wheel will live on. The finish follows from there.

Process

How a commission works.

01
Inquiry

Fill out the form below with your setup, sizing, and what finish you're after. I'll come back within a few days with questions and a rough quote.

02
Disassembly

Every set is fully taken apart — face, lip, and barrel separated, hardware catalogued. Condition is assessed and documented before any work begins.

03
Refinishing

Surface prep, paint or powder, polish on the lip if specified. Finish is matched to your direction — raw, coated, or somewhere between.

04
Reassembly

Rebuilt with new or sourced hardware, torqued to spec, and photographed in detail before they're boxed and shipped back.
